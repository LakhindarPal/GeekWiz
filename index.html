How to play

To play the quiz, follow these steps:

  1. Choose Category: Select a category from the "Select Category" dropdown. Options include Linux, Bash, Docker, SQL, CMS, Code, or DevOps.
  2. Select Difficulty: Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard from the "Select Difficulty" dropdown.
  3. Start Quiz: Click the "Start Quiz" button to begin.
  4. Answer Questions: For each question, read the prompt and select the correct answer.
  5. Next Question: Click "Next Question" to move to the next one.
  6. Complete Quiz: Continue until the end of the quiz.
  7. Finish Before Timeout: You have a total of 5 minutes to complete the quiz. The timer starts when you click "Start Quiz" and stops when you finish playing or time runs out.
  8. View Results: After all questions, view your final score and completion message.
  9. Restart Quiz: Click "Restart Quiz" to play again.

Enjoy playing the programming quiz!
